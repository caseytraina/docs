<Info>
  ### Schema

  - **Input**: The direct URL to a YouTube channel. (e.g. https://youtube.com/@ycombinator)
  - **Output**: The new youtube video
</Info>

When a workflow is activated with a _New Youtube Video_ trigger, we will begin polling the channel **every hour** scanning for new videos. When a new video that is **longer than 5 minutes is found**, we will trigger your workflow to find new clips and notify you.
